Masscopy2Pc.zip	The PC version
An application that copies multiple files from multiple folders giving them a common prefix and
numbering system, thenn placing them in a folder of your choice.
ie      mymusic7.mp3    atune 76.mp3    manicsnumber7.mp3    could be copied to
     trk-24.mp3     trk-25.mp3     trk-26.mp3    into a folder of your choice.
A limit of 2000 is set on the number that can be copied in one pass
PC version. Tested under XP and Vista
